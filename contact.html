Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Our free trial includes full access to all features for 14 days. No credit card required. You can add up to 10 patients and experience the complete platform before making any commitment.

Is my patient data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level security with end-to-end encryption, HIPAA compliance, and regular security audits. Your data is stored in secure, SOC 2 certified data centers.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive API access and pre-built integrations with popular healthcare systems, payment processors, and communication platforms.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, comprehensive documentation, and video tutorials. Enterprise customers get dedicated account managers.

How easy is it to migrate from my current system?

Our migration team will help you transfer all your data seamlessly. We support most common formats and provide free migration assistance for all new customers.

Can I customize the platform for my practice?

Yes! We offer extensive customization options including custom fields, branded interfaces, custom workflows, and white-label solutions for larger practices.
